Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder, commonly known as ADHD, affects millions of adults around the globe. Reading long-form text can often feel overwhelming, as lines seem to blur together and important details slip away. Digital tools, however, can turn a potentially frustrating experience into a smooth and engaging one.

Techniques like bionic reading boldly emphasize the first few letters of words, guiding the eye effortlessly across a page. Color strategies—such as alternating background shades or applying a gentle gradient from line to line—reduce visual monotony, keeping readers anchored. Other supportive methods include widening sentence spacing, introducing a reading ruler that tracks the cursor, and temporarily dimming imagery so that essential paragraphs shine.

FocusReader Pro combines these ideas into a single, customizable package. Whether you prefer subtle assistance or a pronounced transformation, the controls above let you experiment in real-time. Explore, adjust, and discover the combination that helps you read with confidence and clarity.
